Anneli Goeller is a new media artist and MFA candidate at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Their work seeks to expand the dichotomy between the corporeal and virtual while highlighting the potential of digital space as a queer arena. They have been exhibited internationally at institutions such as the Sheila C. Johnson Design Center in New York, Peripheral Forms in Portland, Lithium Gallery in Chicago as well as the Palazzo dei Cartelloni in Florence. In virtual space their work has been featured in The Wrong - New Digital Art Biennale and The Universal Sea.
